Delicious and Nutritious Smoothie Bowl

This smoothie bowl is a perfect breakfast option that's full of vitamins and nutrients to start your day right. Here's the recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries (blueberries, strawberries, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • Granola and fresh fruit for topping

Instructions:

  1. Blend the banana, frozen berries, almond milk, chia seeds, and almond butter in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with granola and fresh fruit of your choice.
  3. Enjoy immediately for a refreshing and healthy start to your day!
